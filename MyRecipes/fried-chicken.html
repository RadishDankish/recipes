Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • salt
  • red chilli
  • lemon
  • ginger garlic paste
  • oil
  • coriander powder
  • cumin powder
  • garam masala
  • cardamom powder
  • kasuri methi powder
  • curd
  • maida

Steps

  1. marinate chicken with lemon, kashmiri red chilli powder, ginger garlic paste
  2. make second marinate with oil,red chilli powder,coriander powder,and all other spice. In the end add curd and ginger garlic paste
  3. add refined flour in large bowl and add salt. add two layers coating one simple and other using eggs or chilled water
  4. now fry them
