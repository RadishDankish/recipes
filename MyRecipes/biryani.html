Biryani

Ingredients

  • onion
  • curd
  • mint leaves
  • green chillies
  • coriander
  • ginger garlic paste
  • oil
  • salt
  • coriander powder
  • red chilli powder
  • turmeric
  • cardamom powder
  • chicken
  • basmati rice
  • cardamom stick
  • bayleaf
  • saffron
  • cloves

Steps

  1. fry the onions light brown
  2. marinate chicken with friend onion,curd,coriander,mint,green chilli,ginger garlic paste,fried oil,salt,coriander powder,turmeric powder.
  3. boil water and add salt, bayleaf, cinnamon stick, ghee, rice.
  4. cover the lid and allow to cook
  5. add marinated chciken, add a layer of uncooked rice, and repeat.
  6. add jhol, saffron water,ghee and fried onion.
